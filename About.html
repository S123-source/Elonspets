Welcome to Elon's Pet Store!

At Elon's Pet Store, we are passionate about helping pet owners find the best products for their beloved companions. Whether you’re looking for food, toys, accessories, or a new pet, we’ve got you covered!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide pet owners with high-quality products and expert advice to help them take care of their furry, feathered, or scaly friends. We believe that pets are family, and we are committed to enhancing their lives and the lives of the people who love them.

Our Story

Elon's Pet Store was founded in April 2024 with the goal of providing pet owners in Kigali with high-quality products and exceptional customer service. Over the years, we’ve become a trusted resource for pet care and continue to grow our selection and services to better meet the needs of our customers and their pets.

Customer Service

We pride ourselves on our friendly and knowledgeable staff who are here to help you find exactly what your pet needs. Whether you’re a first-time pet owner or an experienced one, we’re always available to offer personalized advice and recommendations.
